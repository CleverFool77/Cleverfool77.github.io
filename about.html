Connecting Passion with Purpose

Code wrangler of building resilient edge and cloud systems. Part engineer,
part storyteller—by day,

Beyond work, I’m an avid fan of manga, anime, and Donghua, constantly exploring new stories and worlds. Writing poetry is a deep passion of mine, and I find serenity in staying indoors, allowing creativity to flow. Whether it’s through code or verse, I enjoy bringing ideas to life
